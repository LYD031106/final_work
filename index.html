In order to study the AOX pollution status in Hangzhou Bay sea area (a comprehensive index for evaluating the total amount of organic halides), the experimental group took water samples and surface sediment samples from 12 points in Hangzhou Bay Sea area

In terms of the overall AOX concentration range, AOX concentrations in 2014, 2015 and 2016 ranged from 100.1 to 333.2 μg/L (mean 237.6 μg/L), 150.3 to 760.1 μg/L (mean 433.1 μg/L) and 130.4 to 754.3, respectively μg/L (mean 363.3 μg/L). In other words, the concentration detected in 2015 and 2016 was higher than that detected in 2014, which may be related to the continuous input of AOX

Halogenated aromatic compounds have good stability, fatigue resistance, flame retardant, heat resistance and other characteristics, and are widely used in industrial and agricultural production, such as pesticides, dyes, medicine, co-solvents and flame retardants (Prakash et al., 2011; Cooper et al., 2015).

Reduction dehalogenase is an essential enzyme in organic halide respiration. It consists of catalytic subunit A and membrane protein B and is encoded by the rdhAB gene. There are 32 rdhAB genes in the genome of Dehalococcoides mccartyi CBDB1.

and this high number may reflect the diversity of organic halogenated compounds that can be reduced and dehalogenated by dehalogenated cocci strains.

Previous studies used heterologous expression and in vitro studies to study the control of the expression of two rdhAB genes (cbdbA1453/cbdbA1452 and cbdbA1455/cbdbA1454) by the directly upstream encoded marr type regulator Rdh2R (cbdbA1456).

Transcription of the rdhA gene (cbdbA1455) has previously been shown to be up-regulated in the presence of 1,2, 3-trichlorobenzene (TCB) [1].

The complete dehalogenase operon structure is shown in the figure. In the first step of this project, four bph genes are required in the reaction, and the expression product is diphenyldimethoxase (BPDO).

This enzyme catalyzes BPDO to catalyze the formation of 2, 3-dihydro-2, 3-dihydroxy-dioxygen from the diphenyl ring, which is the first intermediate in the bacterial diphenyl catabolic pathway. BPDO requires four components, including two subunit oxygenases

At the same time, AHDO, the cis-dihydrodiol produced by catalytic oxygenation, can eventually be oxidized by cis-dihydrodiol dehydrogenase in soil bacteria, whereas trans diol metabolites produced solely by plant cytochrome p450 metabolism are not easily further dehydrogenated by soil bacteria.

Lateral Flow Assay Results

To make sure that the test is as convenient as possible, we collaborated with Micromolds to create a 3D model of the lateral flow assay test case. The created case has a specially designed well to add the liquid onto the sample pad and the window meant to observe the results. Also, this case makes sure that the test strip with dried reagents is protected from the environment. The design of this test case was a crucial step for the future implementation of the test in aquaculture farms.

The developed qualitative lateral flow assay test can give three different answers:

Positive

If test and control lines are present.

Negative

If only the control line appears.

No lines / only test line

the test is invalid and must be repeated. This might have happened due to problems of the fluid flow.
